# Customer-Churn-Analysis-
This repository contains a comprehensive Customer Churn Analysis focused on identifying key drivers of customer attrition and providing data-driven retention strategies. The analysis evaluates a dataset of 10,000 customers, examining demographics, financial profiles, and engagement levels.

🚀 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges for subscription-based businesses.

Losing customers directly impacts revenue, growth, and long-term sustainability.

This project focuses on analyzing customer data to understand:

Why customers leave
Which customers are most at risk
What patterns lead to churn

The goal is to transform raw data into actionable business insights.

🎯 Business Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

Calculate overall churn rate
Identify high-risk customer segments
Analyze behavioral and financial patterns
Provide data-driven retention strategies
📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains structured customer-level data including:

Customer ID
Age and demographics
Geography (country)
Account balance
Credit score
Activity status (active/inactive)
Credit card ownership
Estimated salary
Tenure (relationship duration)
Churn status

This allows both descriptive and diagnostic analysis.

🔍 Analysis Breakdown
1️⃣ Customer Overview

We first evaluate the overall dataset to understand scale and churn level.

Total number of customers
Total churned customers
Churn rate percentage

Insight:
A high churn rate (>20%) signals serious retention issues.

2️⃣ Active vs Churned Customers

We compare key financial indicators:

Average balance
Average credit score

Insight:
Churned customers typically show:

Lower account balances
Lower credit scores

This indicates weaker engagement or financial instability.

3️⃣ Demographic Analysis

We segment customers based on:

Gender
Geography
Age groups

Insight:

Certain countries show higher churn rates
Specific age groups (young or senior) churn more
Gender-based patterns may exist

This helps in targeted marketing.

4️⃣ Customer Engagement Analysis

We analyze behavioral indicators:

Active vs inactive members
Credit card ownership

Insight:

Inactive users are significantly more likely to churn
Customers without credit cards show lower engagement
5️⃣ High-Value Customer Risk

We identify customers with:

High balance
High salary
Low activity

Insight:

These customers are extremely important because:

They generate higher revenue
Losing them has a major business impact

They require priority retention strategies.

6️⃣ Churn Trends & Patterns

We analyze churn over time:

Churn by tenure
Country-wise churn trends

Insight:

Customers tend to churn at specific lifecycle stages
Certain regions consistently show higher churn
